Solidity is a brand-new programming language developed by Ethereum, the second-largest cryptocurrency market by capitalization.

The important factors that makes Solidity so special:

1- Functional:- Solidity language is used for smart contracts. 

2- Flexible:- You can use a Remix IDE online compiler or download a command line compiler on your pc to write smart contracts.

3- Promising:- The world spends billions on Blockchain solutions every year.While most of it comes from financial companies, all kinds of business use Blockchain .

4- Improving:- Solidity language update, such as new features and bug fixes, are introduced constantly.
